Why is finding attachments so hard?

Gmail threads collapse messages and nest replies, scattering attachments across multiple messages. That makes it tedious to find and download files, especially when attachments sit in older replies or forwarded content. Users often resort to opening each message individually — a slow, error-prone workflow. Our extension solves this by surfacing every attachment from the whole conversation so you can download all attachments from a Gmail thread in seconds.

How it Works

The extension uses authorized, read-only access to your mailbox metadata to aggregate attachment identifiers and present them in one place — locally in your browser. No message bodies are stored externally.

Instant Visibility

See every attachment in the conversation at a glance. No more opening separate emails — your attachment manager shows filenames, sizes, and senders in one list.
